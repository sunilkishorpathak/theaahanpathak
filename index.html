About Me

Hi! I'm Aahan Pathak, and I'm 7 years old. I'm really excited to share my journey with you!

I go to Faria Academy, which is California's #1 school, and I love learning new things every day. My teachers say I'm doing really well in all my subjects, and I always try my best to be a good student.

But that's not all I do! I started playing tennis when I was 4.5 years old, and it's become one of my favorite things in the world. I practice with amazing tools like the TopSpin Pro and Service Doctor, and I do ladder drills to get faster and stronger. I take group lessons, semi-private lessons, and even private coaching sessions to keep getting better at my forehand, backhand, and topspin shots.

I'm also an advanced swimmer! Swimming helps me stay strong and gives me more endurance for tennis. It's so much fun to be in the water, and I love challenging myself to swim longer distances.

At school, I'm on the running team, and we have these awesome kids vs parents races that everyone talks about! It's so cool to run with my classmates and sometimes even beat the grown-ups.

The best part is that I get to do all these sports while still being a great student. My parents and coaches help me manage my time so I can study hard and play hard too. I want to show other kids that you can be excellent at school AND be a great athlete!
